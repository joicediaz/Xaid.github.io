Ordinary

The Ordinary is a skincare brand known for offering high-quality, science-backed formulations at affordable prices. Their products focus on effective, active ingredients, such as retinoids, peptides, acids, and oils, to address specific skin concerns. The brand is widely recognized for its transparency in ingredients and for avoiding unnecessary fillers in its products.

Some By Mi

Some By Mi-Is a popular Korean skincare brand known for its use of natural ingredients and effective formulations, especially for sensitive and acne-prone skin. Their toners are especially renowned for their ability to address issues like acne, pigmentation, and skin irritation.

Here are the key benefits of Some By Mi toners, particularly their best-known products:

Some By Mi AHA BHA PHA 30 Days Miracle Toner

*Exfoliates and Clears Pores: Contains AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acid), BHA (Beta Hydroxy Acid), and PHA (Polyhydroxy Acid) to exfoliate dead skin cells, unclog pores, and reduce excess oil.

*Improves Skin Texture: Helps smooth rough texture and refine the skin's surface.

*Targets Acne and Breakouts: The formula helps control sebum production, prevent acne, and reduce inflammation.

*Brightens Skin: AHA and PHA help brighten and even out the skin tone over time.

*Soothes and Calms: Contains ingredients like Tea Tree Leaf Water and Centella Asiatica to calm and hydrate irritated skin, reducing redness and inflammation.

Some By Mi- Galactomyces Pure Vitamin C Glow Toner

*Brightens and Evens Skin Tone: Contains Galactomyces Ferment Filtrate and Vitamin C, which help to brighten and improve skin radiance.

*Improves Skin Elasticity: Enhances skin elasticity and firms the skin for a more youthful appearance.

*Hydrates and Nourishes: Provides deep hydration and nourishment, leaving the skin soft and moisturized.

*Targets Hyperpigmentation: Helps reduce the appearance of dark spots, pigmentation, and dullness, promoting a clearer complexion.

Both toners are designed to improve overall skin health, targeting a variety of skin concerns with gentle, yet effective active ingredients.
