olay

Olay is a globally recognized skincare brand known for its science-driven formulas and commitment to effective, accessible skincare. Founded in 1952, Olay offers a wide range of products that target aging, hydration, and skin renewal, using ingredients like peptides, niacinamide, and retinol. The brand is trusted for delivering visible results with gentle, high-performance products suitable for all skin types.

Olay Ultimate Eye Cream is a targeted skincare solution designed to address common concerns around the eyes, including dark circles, puffiness, fine lines, and wrinkles. Its formula combines peptides, niacinamide, hyaluronic acid, and caffeine to hydrate, firm, and brighten the delicate under-eye area.

*Minimizes puffiness with caffeine.

*Smooths fine lines and wrinkles with peptides.

*Hydrates and firms the skin with hyaluronic acid.

* Gentle,safe, and fragrance free for sensitive skin.

tatcha

TATCHA is a luxury skincare brand inspired by Japanese beauty rituals. Known for using natural, high-quality ingredients like green tea and rice, TATCHA creates products that promote hydration, radiance, and healthy skin. The brand combines traditional wisdom with modern science for a luxurious, effective skincare experience.

TATCHA The Dewy Skin Cream is a luxurious, rich moisturizer designed to hydrate and nourish dry or dull skin. Known for its ultra-hydrating formula, this cream provides a dewy, radiant glow while improving skin texture and elasticity.

*Japanese Purple Rice: Rich in antioxidants, it helps to strengthen and replenish skin.

*Vitamin E: Provides intense moisture and helps protect the skin's barrier.

*Peony Extract: Brightens and balances the complexion.

*Ceramides: Support the skin's natural moisture barrier, locking in hydration.

TATCHA The Dewy Skin Cream is a go-to for achieving smooth, glowing, and deeply hydrated skin with a luxurious, rich feel.
