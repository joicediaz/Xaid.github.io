Serums

Cera Ve

CeraVe is a highly regarded skincare brand known for its dermatologist-developed formulas designed to support healthy skin. Launched in 2005, CeraVe quickly gained popularity for its focus on providing essential skincare products with effective ingredients that cater to various skin types and concerns, including dry, oily, sensitive, and acne-prone skin.

CeraVe Resurfacing Retinol Eye Repair Cream is an eye serum designed to target common eye-area concerns like fine lines, wrinkles, puffiness, and dark circles. It combines the power of retinol with nourishing ingredients to help improve the appearance of the delicate skin around the eyes.

*Reduces Fine Lines and Wrinkles: Retinol (a form of Vitamin A) helps stimulate collagen production and accelerate skin turnover, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles over time.

*Brightens Dark Circles: The formula contains licorice extract, known for its brightening properties, which can help reduce the appearance of dark circles around the eyes.

*Improves Skin Texture: Helps smooth out rough or uneven skin texture around the eyes, making the area look more youthful and refreshed.

* Hydrates and Softens: Contains hyaluronic acid and ceramides to keep the skin hydrated, preventing dryness and promoting a smoother, softer under-eye area.

Vichy

Vichy is a French skincare brand renowned for its dermatologist-developed products that focus on promoting skin health and vitality. Founded in 1931, Vichy combines natural ingredients, particularly mineral-rich volcanic water, with advanced scientific formulations. The brand is known for its commitment to addressing a variety of skin concerns, including aging, dryness, sensitivity, and pigmentation, through clinically proven products.

Vichy Liftactiv Vitamin C Brightening Skin Corrector is a potent antioxidant serum designed to improve skin radiance, reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, and protect the skin from environmental damage. It combines a high concentration of vitamin C with other skin-loving ingredients to boost the skin’s overall health and appearance.

*15% Pure Vitamin C: A highly stabilized form of pure vitamin C (ascorbic acid) that delivers effective brightening and anti-aging benefits.

*Vichy Volcanic Water: Rich in minerals, this water helps to strengthen and replenish the skin’s natural defenses.

*Hyaluronic Acid: Helps to hydrate and plump the skin, providing additional moisture.

Protects Against Environmental Damage: As a powerful antioxidant, vitamin C helps protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals and environmental stressors like pollution and UV radiation.
